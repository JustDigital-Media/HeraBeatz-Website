Leasing rights allow the buyer to use the beat for limited recording purposes. You can monetize your song, but the producer retains rights to resell the beat.

Exclusive rights grant the buyer full ownership of the beat. The producer cannot resell the beat, and you get unlimited recording, sales, and broadcast rights.
